Our First Date

A memory I keep on loop

That April day in Vellore will always be one of my favourites. We finally met after months of waiting and the moment we were together it felt like home. We sat in the hotel and clung to each other like we never wanted to leave and we didn't. The hours slipped by but it never felt like time was moving. It was just us lost in our own little world. That day was more than a first date it was a glimpse into our future a promise of many tomorrows.
